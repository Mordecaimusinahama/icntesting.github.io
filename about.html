About Us


ICN consultants was established in 2020, when Mr Irvine and Mr Clifford came into partnership. They both decided to start up a consulting firm after observing how much the construction and engineering
industry incur cost overruns After conducting a research with projects managers, they observed that
construction industries is lagging behind hence through the use of technology ICN has ensure that the project are completed in the required time frame. Although the company is only emerging in the industries all of our clientele are happy to have chose to engage with our services.

ICN consultants delivers innovative solutions to clients across a broad spectrum of capabilities including building, civils, roads and earthworks construction, property development and emerging capabilities within infrastructure operations and maintenance. We leverage these capabilities to deliver large, complex projects as well as simple and small projects and services in rural and urban settings. 

Through the many important services we provide and the innovative, sustainable solutions we use to deliver them, ICN Consultants contributes to the development of communities in which we operate. Our values guide what we do and how we work, and this dual focus helps to ensure a sustainable future for our business, our people, clients, and communities. We focus on the areas where we can make the most significant positive contributions: Broad-Based Black Economic Empowerment (B-BBEE) and Transformation; Health, Safety & Environment; People Development; Diversity; Socio-Economic (Community) Development and Ethical Governance. ICN Consultants is also a wholly black-owned business .ICN Consultancy’s Integrated Management System  (IMS) ensures our divisions adopt the most stringent standards and compliance to ISO 9001, ISO 14001 and OHSAS 18001, in addition to compliance to ours client’s
